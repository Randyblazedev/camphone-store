Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our catalog, click "Buy Now" on any phone, and you'll be redirected to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Complete your order by chatting with our sales team. Payment is made via Mobile Money (MoMo) or cash on delivery.
Do you deliver across Cameroon?
Yes! We deliver to all 10 regions of Cameroon. Delivery takes 24-48 hours for major cities (Douala, Yaoundé, Bamenda, Bafoussam) and 2-5 days for other regions. Delivery fees vary by location.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Orange Money), bank transfer, and cash on delivery. For online orders, we recommend MoMo for the fastest processing.
Do your phones come with warranty?
All new phones come with a 12-month manufacturer warranty. Used phones come with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee — if you're not happy, return it within 30 days for a full refund.
Can I trade in my old phone?
Yes! We offer trade-in options. Contact us on WhatsApp with your phone model and condition, and we'll give you a fair valuation that can be applied toward your new purchase.
